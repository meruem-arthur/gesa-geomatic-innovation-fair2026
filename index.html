SECTION 01

What is the GIF?

The Geomatic Innovative Fair (GIF) is a semester-end showcase where students — individually or in groups — design and present innovative tools, systems, or solutions relevant to Geomatic Engineering.

Entries range from a simple Excel calculation sheet to AI-powered geospatial systems. There are no limits on complexity — what matters is the idea, the effort, and the relevance to the field.

The GIF bridges the gap between classroom theory and real-world innovation, giving every student a platform to demonstrate what they can build.

"From a single spreadsheet to a full geospatial system — every idea has a place at the GIF."

SECTION 03

What Can You Build?

📊
Excel / Spreadsheet Tools
Traverse calculations, area computations, coordinate transformations, data sheets.
📱
Mobile or Web Apps
Field data collection apps, geospatial viewers, survey management tools.
🤖
AI & Robotics
AI-powered geospatial solutions, automated classification systems, robotic aids.
🗺️
GIS & Mapping
Real-world mapping solutions, spatial analysis tools, cartographic outputs.
🔧
Physical Prototypes
Low-cost surveying aids, physical models, instrumentation adaptations.
💻
Scripts & Programs
Automated data processing pipelines, survey utilities, coordinate calculators.

SECTION 04

How It Works

1
Register Your Intent
Fill in the registration form — individually or as a group, print and submit to the HOD'S office — to officially declare your participation in the fair.
FIRST SEMESTER
2
Build Your Project
Develop your idea over the semester break and into Second Semester. Use any tools, languages, or methods you know.
SEMESTER BREAK → SECOND SEMESTER
3
Submit Project Confirmation
Submit your Project Confirmation form at least 2 weeks before the fair date to confirm your slot.
2 WEEKS BEFORE FAIR
4
Present at the GIF
Showcase your project at the Geomatic Innovation Fair. Demonstrate and explain your work to judges and peers.
